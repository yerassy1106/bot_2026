# Pricing Strategy Document

## Executive Summary
Using the outputs from the CFO Bot, we have analyzed the unit economics of our proposed cloud architecture. Our strategy focuses on cost-optimization through precise resource allocation.

## Strategic Justification
1.  **Compute Efficiency**: By using hourly tracking, we avoid over-provisioning.
2.  **Storage Scalability**: Costs are strictly linear based on GB usage, allowing for predictable growth.
3.  **Financial Accuracy**: The use of the CFO Bot ensures that our budget projections align with actual cloud provider billing logic.
